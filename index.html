why is essaouira famous

Essaouira, a captivating "coastal gem" in Morocco, is renowned for its UNESCO-listed, blue-and-white medina, fortified 18th-century seaside ramparts, and reputation as the "Wind City of Africa". It is famous for a relaxed atmosphere, fresh seafood, wind-based water sports, the annual Gnaoua World Music Festival, and its role as a Game of Thrones filming location.
